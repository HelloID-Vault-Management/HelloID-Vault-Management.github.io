# Managing Contacts

Contacts are managed from the person detail **Contacts** tab.

## Contact Types

Each person can have two types of contacts:
- **Personal** — Personal contact information
- **Business** — Business/work contact information

## Adding a Contact

1. Select a person
2. Go to the **Contacts** tab
3. Click **Add Contact**
4. Choose type: **Personal** or **Business**
5. Fill in the fields:
   - Email
   - Phone (mobile and fixed)
   - Address (street, house number, postal code, city, country)
6. Click **Save**

## Editing a Contact

1. Go to the person's **Contacts** tab
2. Click **Edit** on the contact you want to modify
3. Update the fields
4. Click **Save**

## Deleting a Contact

1. Go to the person's **Contacts** tab
2. Click **Delete** on the contact
3. Confirm the deletion

## Global Contacts View

The **Contacts** item in the sidebar shows all contacts across all persons in a single DataGrid. This is useful for bulk review and searching.
